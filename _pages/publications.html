Biography

Yifan Chen received his B.S. degree in Computer Science from Peking University, Beijing, China, in 2023. He is currently a second-year Ph.D. student at Peking University, advised by Prof. Yibo Lin. His research focuses on machine learning and large-scale optimization for Electronic Design Automation (EDA), with an emphasis on placement, routing, and routability-driven physical design.
